## Resolution 1: Approval of New ETF

**WHEREAS**, the Investment Adviser has presented to the Board a proposal for the organization and launch of a new exchange-traded fund, `{{fund_name}}` (the "Fund");

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed the Fund's proposed investment objective, strategies, risks, and anticipated expense structure;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has considered the potential benefits to shareholders and the competitive landscape for the proposed Fund;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed management's analysis of the market opportunity and financial projections;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Board of Directors hereby approves the establishment of `{{fund_name}}`, a new series of `{{trust_name}}`, as an exchange-traded fund;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Fund shall have the following key terms:

| Term                 | Specification                   |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Investment Objective | `{{investment_objective}}`      |
| Benchmark Index      | `{{benchmark_index}}`           |
| Target Expense Ratio | `{{expense_ratio}}` bps         |
| Creation Unit Size   | `{{creation_unit_size}}` shares |
| Primary Exchange     | `{{exchange}}`                  |

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the officers of the Trust are hereby authorized and directed to execute and file, or cause to be filed, all documents necessary to register the Fund, including but not limited to amendments to the Trust's registration statement on Form N-1A.

## Resolution 2: Approval of Investment Advisory Agreement

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed and considered the terms of the Investment Advisory Agreement (the "Agreement") between the Trust, on behalf of the Fund, and `{{adviser_name}}` (the "Adviser");

**WHEREAS**, the Board has considered the factors required under Section 15(c) of the Investment Company Act of 1940, including:

1. **Nature, extent, and quality of services** to be provided by the Adviser;
2. **Investment performance** of the Adviser and comparable funds;
3. **Cost of services and profitability** to the Adviser;
4. **Economies of scale** and extent to which they are shared with shareholders;
5. **Comparison of fees** with those charged to comparable funds;

**WHEREAS**, the Independent Directors met in executive session to discuss these factors;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Investment Advisory Agreement between the Trust, on behalf of `{{fund_name}}`, and `{{adviser_name}}`, substantially in the form presented to the Board, is hereby approved;

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the management fee under the Agreement shall be `{{management_fee}}` basis points per annum of the Fund's average daily net assets.
