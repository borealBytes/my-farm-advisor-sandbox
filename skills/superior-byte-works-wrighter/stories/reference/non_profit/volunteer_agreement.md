## 3. Volunteer Relationship

### Nature of Relationship

The Volunteer is providing services on a voluntary basis without expectation of compensation. This Agreement does not create an employment relationship, and the Volunteer is not an employee, contractor, or agent of the Organization.

**The Volunteer understands that:**

- No wages, salary, or benefits will be provided
- Workers' compensation coverage may be limited or not applicable
- Unemployment benefits are not available
- The Organization is not responsible for tax withholding

### At-Will Relationship

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice.
