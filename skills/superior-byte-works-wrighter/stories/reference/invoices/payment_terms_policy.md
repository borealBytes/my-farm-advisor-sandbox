## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the standard payment terms, conditions, and procedures governing all customer invoices issued by [Company Name]. It ensures consistent application of credit terms, defines acceptable payment methods, and outlines the escalation process for delinquent accounts. All personnel involved in quoting, contracting, invoicing, or collections must adhere to this policy.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All commercial invoices issued to customers
- All accounts receivable balances
- All payment arrangements and extensions
- All credit terms offered during the sales cycle

**Exclusions:** Intercompany transactions, employee expense reimbursements, and vendor refunds are governed by separate policies.

## 3. Standard Payment Terms

### 3.1 Term Definitions

| Customer Tier     | Net Terms      | Early Payment Discount | Late Fee Rate           | Grace Period |
| ----------------- | -------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------ |
| Standard          | Net 30         | 2/10 Net 30            | 1.5%/month              | 5 days       |
| Enterprise        | Net 45         | 1/10 Net 45            | 1.5%/month              | 7 days       |
| Government        | Net 60         | None                   | Per contract            | 10 days      |
| Strategic Partner | Net 30         | 2/10 Net 30            | Waived (1st occurrence) | 5 days       |
| Prepaid           | Due on receipt | 5% discount            | N/A                     | N/A          |

### 3.2 Term Assignment Criteria

| Criteria                 | Standard   | Enterprise          | Government     | Strategic |
| ------------------------ | ---------- | ------------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| Annual revenue threshold | < $100K    | $100K–$1M           | N/A            | > $1M     |
| Credit score minimum     | 650        | 700                 | N/A            | 700       |
| Payment history required | None       | 6 months            | N/A            | 12 months |
| Contract term minimum    | None       | 12 months           | Per regulation | 24 months |
| Approval authority       | AR Manager | Director of Finance | CFO            | CFO       |

### 3.3 Early Payment Discounts

Early payment discounts are expressed as **[Discount%]/[Days] Net [Terms]**. For example, **2/10 Net 30** means a 2% discount is available if payment is received within 10 days; otherwise, the full amount is due in 30 days.

- Discounts apply to the invoice subtotal before taxes
- Partial payments do not qualify for early payment discounts
- Discount eligibility expires at 11:59 PM local time on the discount deadline

## 5. Credit Evaluation and Approval

### 5.1 New Customer Credit Review

All new customers requesting payment terms beyond "Due on Receipt" must undergo a credit evaluation:

1. Customer completes Credit Application Form (FIN-CA-001)
2. AR team obtains commercial credit report (Dun & Bradstreet or equivalent)
3. AR Manager reviews and assigns preliminary credit limit
4. Director of Finance approves limits exceeding $50,000
5. CFO approves limits exceeding $250,000

### 5.2 Credit Limit Schedule

| Risk Rating   | Credit Limit Range   | Review Frequency |
| ------------- | -------------------- | ---------------- |
| Low Risk      | $100,000–$500,000    | Annual           |
| Moderate Risk | $25,000–$100,000     | Semi-annual      |
| High Risk     | $5,000–$25,000       | Quarterly        |
| New/Unrated   | $5,000 (provisional) | After 90 days    |

### 5.3 Ongoing Credit Monitoring

- Credit reviews are conducted at the frequency indicated by risk rating
- Any payment delinquency of 60+ days triggers an immediate credit review
- Material changes in customer financial condition require re-evaluation
- Customer requests for increased credit limits require updated financials

## 6. Late Payment Policy

### 6.1 Escalation Schedule

| Days Past Due | Action                                    | Responsible Party   | Communication    |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------- | ---------------- |
| 1–15 days     | Courtesy reminder                         | Automated system    | Email            |
| 16–30 days    | First formal notice                       | AR Representative   | Email + phone    |
| 31–45 days    | Second notice; account hold warning       | AR Manager          | Email + phone    |
| 46–60 days    | Account placed on credit hold             | AR Manager          | Certified letter |
| 61–90 days    | Final demand; collections warning         | Director of Finance | Certified letter |
| 91+ days      | Referred to external collections or legal | CFO                 | Formal demand    |

### 6.2 Late Fee Calculation

Late fees are calculated as follows:

- **Rate:** 1.5% per month (18% per annum) on the outstanding balance
- **Accrual start:** First day after the grace period expires
- **Compounding:** Simple interest; does not compound
- **Cap:** Late fees shall not exceed the maximum rate permitted by applicable law
- **Waiver:** First occurrence may be waived for Strategic Partner accounts with CFO approval

### 6.3 Account Hold Procedures

When an account is placed on credit hold:

- No new orders will be processed
- Existing shipments in transit will be delivered
- Customer is notified in writing within one business day
- Hold is lifted within 24 hours of payment receipt
- Sales team is notified concurrently

## 7. Payment Disputes

All payment disputes must be submitted in writing within **15 business days** of invoice receipt. The Invoice Dispute Resolution Process (FIN-DR-[XXX]) governs dispute handling. Disputed amounts do not accrue late fees while under active investigation, provided the dispute is submitted in good faith.

## 8. Exceptions and Modifications

### 8.1 Non-Standard Terms

Requests for payment terms outside this policy require:

| Deviation                       | Approval Required   |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Terms extended by up to 15 days | AR Manager          |
| Terms extended by 16–30 days    | Director of Finance |
| Terms extended by 31+ days      | CFO                 |
| Late fee waiver (non-Strategic) | Director of Finance |
| Credit limit override           | CFO                 |

### 8.2 Documentation Requirements

All exceptions must be documented with:

- Written justification from the requesting party
- Risk assessment from the AR team
- Approval signature from the appropriate authority
- Expiration date for the exception (maximum 12 months)
