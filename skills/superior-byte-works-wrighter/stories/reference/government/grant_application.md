## Project Summary/Abstract

### Executive Summary (250 words maximum)

Provide a clear, concise summary of:

- Problem statement and significance
- Project goals and objectives
- Target population and geographic area
- Expected outcomes and impact
- Total budget amount

> [!TIP]
> Write for a general audience. Avoid jargon and technical terminology. This section may be published.

## Project Narrative

### Statement of Need

Describe the problem or need your project addresses:

1. **Problem Identification**
   - Evidence supporting the need
   - Affected population demographics
   - Geographic scope and context
   - Current gaps in services or knowledge

2. **Significance**
   - Why this problem matters
   - Consequences of inaction
   - Alignment with agency priorities
   - Potential for broader impact

### Budget Narrative

> [!WARNING]
> Costs must be necessary, reasonable, allocable, and allowable per 2 CFR 200. Provide detailed justification for each line item.

**Personnel:**

- Name/Position, % effort, salary, fringe rate
- Role in project
- Calculation methodology

**Equipment:**

- Item description and cost
- Justification for purchase vs. lease
- Disposition plan after project

**Subawards/Contracts:**

- Scope of work
- Selection criteria
- Cost reasonableness
