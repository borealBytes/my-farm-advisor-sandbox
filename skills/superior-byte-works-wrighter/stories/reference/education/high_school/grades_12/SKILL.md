# Grade 12 Templates

Templates for senior year and transition to post-secondary.

## Areas Covered

- **College Applications**: Common App, supplements
- **Financial Aid**: FAFSA, scholarships, aid
- **Senior Year**: Keeping grades up
- **Decision Making**: Comparing offers
- **Transition**: College prep, gap year
- **Reflection**: Senior capstone

## Senior Focus

- Completing applications
- Securing financial aid
- Making final decisions
- Preparing for transition
- Celebrating achievements
