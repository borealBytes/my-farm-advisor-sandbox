## Context Clues

### Types of Context Clues

**1. Definition:**
"Photosynthesis, the process by which plants make food using sunlight..."
- Clue: comma + definition
- Meaning: {{MEANING_1}}

**2. Example:**
"Mammals, such as dogs and cats, are warm-blooded."
- Clue: such as
- Meaning: {{MEANING_2}}

**3. Synonym:**
"The meal was delectable, or very delicious."
- Clue: or
- Meaning: {{MEANING_3}}

**4. Antonym:**
"Unlike her gregarious sister, Maria was shy."
- Clue: unlike
- Meaning: {{MEANING_4}}

**5. Explanation:**
"He was famished after not eating for two days."
- Clue: explanation after
- Meaning: {{MEANING_5}}

**6. General Context:**
"The inundation caused by the flood destroyed many homes."
- Clue: flood context
- Meaning: {{MEANING_6}}

## Learning Strategies

### Strategy 1: The 10-Second Rule

**When you see an unknown word:**
1. Try context clues (5 seconds)
2. Mark word to look up later
3. Keep reading
4. Look up after reading

**Don't:** Stop reading to look up every word
