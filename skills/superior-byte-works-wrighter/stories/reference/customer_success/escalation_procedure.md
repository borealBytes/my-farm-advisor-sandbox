## Purpose

Standard process for escalating customer issues.

---

## Escalation Levels

### Level 1: CSM (Day 1)

- First point of contact
- Attempt resolution
- Document in CRM

**Threshold:** Any customer issue

### Level 2: Manager (Day 2-3)

- If CSM cannot resolve
- Involve customer success manager
- Coordinate resources

**Threshold:** Unresolved after 24 hours

### Level 3: Director (Day 3-5)

- Director involvement
- Cross-functional coordination
- Executive briefing

**Threshold:** Unresolved after 72 hours

### Level 4: VP/Executive (Day 5+)

- Executive sponsor assigned
- Emergency response team
- Contract review possible

**Threshold:** Risk of churn or legal action

---

## Escalation Criteria

| Severity | Criteria | Response Time |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| P1 - Critical | Outage, data loss | 1 hour |
| P2 - High | Major feature broken | 4 hours |
| P3 - Medium | Workaround available | 24 hours |
| P4 - Low | Cosmetic issues | 72 hours |

| Level | Customer Communication | Internal Communication |
|-------|------------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Direct from CSM | CSM to manager |
| 2 | Manager involvement | Daily standup |
| 3 | Director outreach | Executive alert |
| 4 | VP call | Crisis team |
