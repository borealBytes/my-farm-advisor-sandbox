## Scope of Services

### Investment Policy Statement Development

The Consultant shall assist the Plan Sponsor in developing and maintaining an Investment Policy Statement (IPS) that includes:

- Investment objectives and goals
- Risk tolerance and time horizon
- Asset allocation guidelines
- Investment manager selection criteria
- Performance monitoring standards

### Manager Search and Selection

> [!TIP]
> The Consultant will conduct thorough due diligence on all recommended investment managers.

The Consultant shall:

- [ ] Identify potential investment managers
- [ ] Conduct quantitative and qualitative analysis
- [ ] Present recommendations to the Plan Sponsor
- [ ] Assist with manager transitions

### Performance Monitoring

| Monitoring Activity     | Frequency     |
| ----------------------- | ------------- |
| Performance Reports     | Quarterly     |
| Manager Review Meetings | Semi-annually |
| Asset Allocation Review | Annually      |
| IPS Review              | Annually      |

### Fiduciary Support

> [!NOTE]
> Under ERISA, the Consultant may serve as a 3(21) or 3(38) fiduciary depending on the appointment terms.

The Consultant acknowledges its fiduciary status:

- [ ] Section 3(21) Co-Fiduciary (investment advice)
- [ ] Section 3(38) Investment Manager (discretionary authority)
- [ ] Non-fiduciary consultant
