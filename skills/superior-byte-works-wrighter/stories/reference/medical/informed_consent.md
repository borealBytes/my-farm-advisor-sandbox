## Will My Information Be Kept Private?

> [!IMPORTANT]
> We will protect your privacy to the extent permitted by law. However, complete confidentiality cannot be guaranteed.

### How We Protect Your Information

- We will use a study number instead of your name on study records
- Your name and other identifying information will be kept separate from study data
- Only authorized study staff will have access to your information
- All records will be stored securely

### Who Will See My Information?

Your information may be reviewed by:

- The study doctor and study staff
- The Institutional Review Board (IRB) that oversees this study
- Government agencies (FDA, NIH) that oversee research
- The study sponsor [Sponsor name]

### HIPAA Authorization

By signing this form, you authorize us to use and disclose your protected health information (PHI) for this research study. This authorization does not expire.

**You have the right to:**

- Revoke this authorization in writing (this will not affect data already collected)
- Request a copy of this authorization
- Refuse to sign this authorization (but then you cannot participate in the study)
