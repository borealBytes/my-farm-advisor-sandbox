## Article IX — General Provisions

### Section 9.1 — Effect on Original Agreement

Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms, conditions, covenants, representations, and warranties of the Original Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and are hereby ratified and confirmed. In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Amendment and the terms of the Original Agreement, the terms of this Amendment shall prevail.

### Section 9.2 — References to Agreement

From and after the Amendment Effective Date, all references to the "Agreement" in the Original Agreement or in any other document executed in connection therewith shall be deemed to refer to the Original Agreement as amended by this Amendment.

### Section 9.3 — Entire Amendment

This Amendment constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements relating to the amendments set forth herein.

### Section 9.4 — Further Amendments

This Amendment may not be further amended or modified except by a written instrument signed by both Parties.

### Section 9.5 — Severability

If any provision of this Amendment is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions of both this Amendment and the Original Agreement shall remain in full force and effect.

### Section 9.6 — Governing Law

This Amendment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the same governing law provisions as set forth in the Original Agreement.

### Section 9.7 — Counterparts

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument. Electronic signatures and PDF copies shall be deemed valid and binding as originals.

### Section 9.8 — Notices

All notices under this Amendment shall be delivered in accordance with the notice provisions of the Original Agreement, or to such updated addresses as the Parties may designate in writing.
