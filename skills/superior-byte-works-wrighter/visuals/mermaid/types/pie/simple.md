# Pie Chart — Simple (2–4 slices)

Flat chart, no styling needed. Use for quick proportions and single metrics.

---

## Example: Budget Allocation

```mermaid
pie
 accTitle: Quarterly Budget Allocation
 accDescr: Simple budget breakdown showing allocation across four major categories

 "📊 Analytics" : 35
 "🔧 Infrastructure" : 25
 "👥 Personnel" : 30
 "📱 Marketing" : 10
```

---

## Example: Task Status Distribution

```mermaid
pie showData
 accTitle: Sprint Task Status Distribution
 accDescr: Current sprint showing percentage of tasks by completion status

 "✅ Done" : 12
 "🔄 In Progress" : 8
 "📋 Todo" : 5
```

## Copy-Paste Template

```mermaid
pie showData
 accTitle: REPLACE WITH 3-8 WORD TITLE
 accDescr: REPLACE WITH 1-2 sentences describing what this chart shows

 "📊 Category A" : 40
 "🔧 Category B" : 30
 "👥 Category C" : 20
 "📱 Category D" : 10
```

---

## Tips

- Use `showData` to display percentages on the chart
- 2–4 slices is ideal for simple charts
- Order by size (largest first) for visual clarity
- Emojis help distinguish categories at a glance
