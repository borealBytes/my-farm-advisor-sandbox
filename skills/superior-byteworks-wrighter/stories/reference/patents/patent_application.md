## Claims

> **Claim Drafting Notes:**
>
> - Independent claims define the broadest scope of protection sought
> - Dependent claims add limitations and provide fallback positions
> - Method claims, system claims, and CRM claims may cover the same inventive concept
> - Claim language must find clear antecedent basis in the specification

### Independent Claims

**Claim 1.** A method for [performing X], comprising:

(a) [first step — use gerund form, e.g., "receiving," "determining," "generating"];

(b) [second step];

(c) [third step]; and

(d) [fourth step],

wherein [optional limiting clause].

**Claim [N].** A system for [performing X], comprising:

a [first component] configured to [function];

a [second component] coupled to the [first component] and configured to [function]; and

a [third component] configured to [function],

wherein [optional limiting clause].

**Claim [N].** A non-transitory computer-readable medium storing instructions that, when executed by a processor, cause the processor to perform operations comprising:

[first operation];

[second operation]; and

[third operation].

### Dependent Claims

**Claim 2.** The method of claim 1, wherein [additional limitation on step (a)].

**Claim 3.** The method of claim 1, further comprising [additional step].

**Claim 4.** The method of claim 1, wherein [element] comprises [specific sub-element].

**Claim 5.** The method of claim 2, wherein [further refinement].

[Continue dependent claims as needed to create a claim tree with multiple fallback positions.]

## Filing Checklist

### Required Components (Non-Provisional)

- [ ] Specification (description, claims, abstract)
- [ ] At least one claim (37 CFR § 1.75)
- [ ] Abstract (37 CFR § 1.72(b), ≤150 words)
- [ ] Drawings (if necessary, 37 CFR § 1.81)
- [ ] Oath/Declaration (37 CFR § 1.63) or Substitute Statement (37 CFR § 1.64)
- [ ] Filing fee (37 CFR § 1.16)
- [ ] Application Data Sheet (37 CFR § 1.76)
- [ ] Information Disclosure Statement (37 CFR § 1.97-1.98)
- [ ] Sequence listing (if applicable, 37 CFR § 1.821-1.825)
- [ ] Assignment (if applicable)
- [ ] Power of Attorney (37 CFR § 1.32)

### Required Components (Provisional)

- [ ] Specification (description only; claims optional)
- [ ] Cover sheet (37 CFR § 1.51(c)(1))
- [ ] Filing fee (37 CFR § 1.16(d))
- [ ] Drawings (if necessary)
