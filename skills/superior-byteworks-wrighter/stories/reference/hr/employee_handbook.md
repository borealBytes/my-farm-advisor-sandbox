## Section 1: Employment Policies

### 1.1 At-Will Employment

Employment with [Company Name] is on an at-will basis. Either the employee or the Company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice, except as otherwise prohibited by law.

### 1.2 Equal Employment Opportunity

[Company Name] is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable law.

### 1.3 Anti-Harassment and Anti-Discrimination

[Company Name] maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to, sexual harassment, racial harassment, and harassment based on any protected characteristic.

**Reporting Procedure**:

1. Report to your direct supervisor or manager
2. If the supervisor is involved, report to HR directly
3. Contact the anonymous ethics hotline at [Phone/URL]
4. File a complaint with [EEOC / State Agency] if unresolved

All reports will be investigated promptly, thoroughly, and confidentially to the extent possible.

### 1.4 Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Compliance

[Company Name] complies with the ADA and applicable state and local laws. Reasonable accommodations will be provided to qualified individuals with disabilities to enable them to perform the essential functions of their position. Employees seeking accommodation should contact Human Resources.

### 1.5 Immigration Law Compliance

[Company Name] is committed to employing only individuals authorized to work in [Country]. All employees must complete Form I-9 and provide acceptable documentation of identity and work authorization within three business days of their start date.

## Section 6: Workplace Conduct

### 6.1 Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to conduct themselves professionally and ethically. This includes:

- Treating colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity
- Maintaining honesty and integrity in all business dealings
- Protecting company assets and confidential information
- Complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies
- Avoiding conflicts of interest or the appearance thereof

### 6.2 Attendance and Punctuality

Regular and punctual attendance is an essential function of every position. Employees must notify their supervisor as soon as practicable when they will be absent or late. Excessive absenteeism or tardiness may result in disciplinary action.

### 6.3 Dress Code

[Company Name] maintains a [professional / business casual / casual] dress code. Employees are expected to present a neat, clean, and professional appearance appropriate to their role and work environment.

### 6.4 Substance Abuse

[Company Name] is committed to maintaining a drug-free workplace. The use, possession, distribution, or sale of illegal drugs or unauthorized controlled substances on company premises or during work hours is strictly prohibited. Reporting to work under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances is prohibited and may result in immediate termination.

### 6.5 Workplace Violence Prevention

[Company Name] has zero tolerance for workplace violence, threats, intimidation, or any conduct that creates a hostile or unsafe work environment. Employees must report any concerns immediately to their supervisor, HR, or security.

## Section 7: Technology and Information Security

### 7.1 Acceptable Use

Company technology resources — including computers, email, internet access, and software — are provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work responsibilities or violate company policy.

### 7.2 Confidentiality and Data Protection

Employees must protect confidential company information, customer data, and trade secrets. Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information is grounds for disciplinary action, up to and including termination, and may result in legal action.

### 7.3 Social Media

Employees are permitted to use personal social media but must not disclose confidential company information, speak on behalf of the company without authorization, or engage in conduct that reflects negatively on [Company Name].

## Section 8: Safety and Health

### 8.1 Workplace Safety

[Company Name] is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment in compliance with OSHA and applicable state regulations. Employees must report all workplace injuries, accidents, or unsafe conditions immediately to their supervisor and HR.

### 8.2 Workers' Compensation

Employees who sustain work-related injuries or illnesses are covered under workers' compensation insurance. Report all injuries immediately, regardless of severity.
