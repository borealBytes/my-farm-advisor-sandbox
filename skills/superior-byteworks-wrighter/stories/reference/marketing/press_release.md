## Pre-Release Checklist

- [ ] News hook validated — is this genuinely newsworthy?
- [ ] Key message aligned with campaign brief / brand positioning
- [ ] All facts, figures, and claims verified by source departments
- [ ] Executive quotes approved by quoted individuals
- [ ] Customer / partner quotes approved and signed off
- [ ] Legal review complete — no IP, liability, or regulatory issues
- [ ] Competitive positioning reviewed — no disparagement or inaccuracies
- [ ] Boilerplate current and consistent with corporate.com
- [ ] Media contact info current
- [ ] Distribution list finalized (wire service, media targets, analysts)
- [ ] Embargo terms communicated to all recipients
- [ ] Supporting assets ready (images, video, data sheet, FAQ)
- [ ] Website newsroom / blog post staged
- [ ] Social amplification copy drafted
- [ ] Internal communications drafted for employees

## Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

### [HEADLINE — All Caps or Title Case, Active Verb, Newsworthy]

**[Subheadline — one sentence elaborating on the headline with supporting detail or data point]**

**[CITY, STATE — Month DD, YYYY]** — [Company Name] ([stock ticker, if public]), [one-line company descriptor], today announced [core news — what happened, what launched, what partnership was formed]. [One sentence on why this matters to the market / customers.]

[Second paragraph: additional context. Expand on the news — the problem being solved, market opportunity, scope of the announcement. Include a relevant data point or market stat to anchor significance.]

"[Executive quote — 2-3 sentences conveying vision, excitement, or strategic rationale]," said [Full Name], [Title] at [Company Name]. "[Continue quote — forward-looking statement about impact or commitment.]"

[Third paragraph: details on product capabilities, partnership terms, program specifics, or research findings. Be specific — features, availability, pricing if applicable, geographic scope.]

[Fourth paragraph (optional): customer or partner perspective, or additional use-case context.]

"[Customer / Partner quote — 2-3 sentences from an external voice validating the announcement]," said [Full Name], [Title] at [Organization Name]. "[Continue quote — specific benefit or result experienced.]"

[Fifth paragraph: availability and call to action. When can people access this? Where do they go? What's the next step?]

**Availability:**
[Product / service] is available [now / starting DD-MMM-YYYY] [in these regions / globally]. [Pricing / access details.] For more information, visit [URL].
