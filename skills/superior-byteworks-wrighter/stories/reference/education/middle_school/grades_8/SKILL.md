# Grade 8 Templates

Templates for third-year middle school students preparing for high school.

## Areas Covered

- **High School Readiness**: Academic and social preparation
- **Advanced Academics**: Pre-algebra, algebra, advanced ELA
- **Career Exploration**: Interests, pathways, planning
- **Civic Engagement**: Service learning, democracy
- **Leadership**: Mentoring, student government, initiatives

## Template Structure

Each template includes:
- High school transition components
- Career connection opportunities
- Student choice and autonomy
- Community engagement options
- Reflection and goal-setting
