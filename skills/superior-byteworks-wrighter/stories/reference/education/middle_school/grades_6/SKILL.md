# Grade 6 Templates

Templates for first-year middle school students transitioning from elementary.

## Areas Covered

- **Transition Support**: Adjusting to middle school expectations
- **Organization**: Multiple classes, lockers, schedules
- **Academic Skills**: Note-taking, studying, time management
- **Social-Emotional**: Peer relationships, identity development
- **Core Subjects**: Math, ELA, Science, Social Studies

## Template Structure

Each template includes:
- Learning objectives aligned to Common Core/State standards
- Differentiation strategies for diverse learners
- Assessment rubrics
- Parent communication components
- Social-emotional learning integration
