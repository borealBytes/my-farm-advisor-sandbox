# GED Science Study Guide

## Test Overview

**Format:**
- Time: 90 minutes
- Questions: 40 multiple choice
- Calculator: Allowed (TI-30XS onscreen)
- Passing: 145+ (out of 200)

**Content Areas:**
1. Life Science (40%)
2. Physical Science (40%)
3. Earth and Space Science (20%)

**Question Types:**
- Multiple choice
- Drag and drop
- Hot spot
- Fill-in-the-blank
- Short answer

## Life Science (40%)

### Cell Biology

**Cell Structure:**
- Nucleus: Control center, contains DNA
- Cytoplasm: Gel-like substance
- Cell membrane: Controls what enters/exits
- Mitochondria: Energy production
- Ribosomes: Protein synthesis
- {{CELL_STRUCTURE}}

**Cell Processes:**
- Photosynthesis: Plants make food
  - Formula: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
  - Location: Chloroplasts
- Cellular respiration: Energy release
  - Formula: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + energy
- {{CELL_PROCESSES}}

**Cell Division:**
- Mitosis: Body cells, 2 identical daughter cells
- Meiosis: Sex cells, 4 cells with half DNA
- {{CELL_DIVISION}}

### Genetics

**DNA:**
- Structure: Double helix
- Bases: A-T, C-G
- Function: Genetic code

**Genes:**
- Dominant: Expressed when present
- Recessive: Only expressed when paired
- Punnett squares: Predict offspring

**Practice:**
Cross: {{TRAIT_1}} × {{TRAIT_2}}
Offspring ratio: {{RATIO}}

### Human Body Systems

**Major Systems:**
- Circulatory: Heart, blood, vessels
- Respiratory: Lungs, breathing
- Digestive: Break down food
- Nervous: Brain, nerves
- Skeletal: Bones, support
- Muscular: Movement
- Immune: Fight disease
- Endocrine: Hormones
- {{BODY_SYSTEMS}}

**Practice:**
System: {{SYSTEM}}
Function: {{FUNCTION}}
Major organs: {{ORGANS}}

### Evolution

**Natural Selection:**
- Variation exists
- Some traits advantageous
- Better survival/reproduction
- Traits passed on
- Population changes over time

**Evidence:**
- Fossil record
- Similar anatomy
- DNA similarities
- {{EVOLUTION_EVIDENCE}}

## Physical Science (40%)

### Chemistry

**States of Matter:**
- Solid: Fixed shape/volume
- Liquid: Fixed volume, takes shape
- Gas: Takes shape/volume
- Plasma: Ionized gas

**Changes:**
- Physical: Change state, same substance
- Chemical: New substance formed
- {{MATTER_CHANGES}}

**Atoms:**
- Protons: Positive, in nucleus
- Neutrons: Neutral, in nucleus
- Electrons: Negative, orbit
- {{ATOM_STRUCTURE}}

**Periodic Table:**
- Periods: Rows
- Groups: Columns, similar properties
- Metals: Left side
- Nonmetals: Right side
- {{PERIODIC_TRENDS}}

**Chemical Bonds:**
- Ionic: Transfer electrons
- Covalent: Share electrons
- {{BOND_TYPES}}

**Chemical Reactions:**
- Reactants → Products
- Balanced equations
- Energy changes
- {{REACTIONS}}

### Physics

**Motion:**
- Speed: Distance/time
- Velocity: Speed + direction
- Acceleration: Change in velocity
- Formula: d = vt

**Force:**
- Newton's Laws:
  1. Inertia: Objects stay in motion/rest
  2. F = ma
  3. Action-reaction
- {{FORCES}}

**Energy:**
- Kinetic: Motion
- Potential: Stored
- Conservation: Energy transforms, not created/destroyed
- {{ENERGY}}

**Waves:**
- Sound: Compression waves
- Light: Electromagnetic
- Frequency: Waves per second
- Wavelength: Distance between waves
- {{WAVES}}

**Electricity:**
- Current: Flow of electrons
- Voltage: Electrical pressure
- Resistance: Opposition to flow
- Ohm's Law: V = IR
- {{ELECTRICITY}}

## Earth and Space Science (20%)

### Earth Structure

**Layers:**
- Crust: Thin, solid
- Mantle: Thick, semi-solid
- Core: Outer (liquid), Inner (solid)
- {{EARTH_LAYERS}}

**Plate Tectonics:**
- Plates move slowly
- Boundaries:
  - Divergent: Move apart
  - Convergent: Move together
  - Transform: Slide past
- {{TECTONICS}}

**Rocks:**
- Igneous: Cooled magma
- Sedimentary: Layers, fossils
- Metamorphic: Changed by heat/pressure
- {{ROCK_CYCLE}}

### Weather and Climate

**Weather:**
- Short-term conditions
- Temperature, precipitation, wind
- Fronts: Cold, warm, stationary
- {{WEATHER}}

**Climate:**
- Long-term patterns
- Factors: Latitude, altitude, ocean currents
- Biomes: Desert, forest, tundra, etc.
- {{CLIMATE}}

**Water Cycle:**
- Evaporation
- Condensation
- Precipitation
- Collection
- {{WATER_CYCLE}}

### Space Science

**Solar System:**
- Sun: Star at center
- Planets: Orbit sun
- Moons: Orbit planets
- Asteroids, comets, meteors
- {{SOLAR_SYSTEM}}

**Stars:**
- Life cycle: Birth → Main sequence → Death
- Types based on temperature/color
- Constellations: Patterns
- {{STARS}}

**Galaxies:**
- Milky Way: Our galaxy
- Spiral, elliptical, irregular
- Universe: Expanding
- {{GALAXIES}}

## Scientific Method

**Steps:**
1. Question
2. Research
3. Hypothesis
4. Experiment
5. Analyze
6. Conclude
7. Communicate

**Variables:**
- Independent: Changed by experimenter
- Dependent: Measured/observed
- Controlled: Kept constant
- {{VARIABLES}}

**Data Analysis:**
- Tables: Organize
- Graphs: Visualize
  - Line: Trends over time
  - Bar: Compare
  - Pie: Parts of whole
- Trends: Patterns
- {{DATA}}
