## Writing Emails

### Basic Email

**Parts:**
- **To:** Recipient's email address
- **Subject:** What email is about
- **Body:** Your message
- **Signature:** Your name/contact

**Template:**

```
To: {{RECIPIENT_EMAIL}}
Subject: {{SUBJECT_LINE}}

Dear {{NAME}},

{{OPENING_SENTENCE}}

{{MESSAGE_BODY}}

{{CLOSING}},
{{YOUR_NAME}}
{{PHONE}}
{{EMAIL}}
```

### Subject Lines

**Good Subject Lines:**
- Clear and specific
- "Question about assignment"
- "Meeting tomorrow at 2pm"
- "Application for position"

**Bad Subject Lines:**
- Too vague: "Hi" or "Question"
- Too long
- ALL CAPS
- No subject

**Your Subject:** {{SUBJECT_EXAMPLE}}

### Email Body

**Opening:**
- Formal: "Dear Mr./Ms. [Name],"
- Casual: "Hi [Name],"
- Very casual: "Hello," or "Hi there,"

**Message:**
- Be clear and concise
- One main point
- Short paragraphs
- Use white space

**Closing:**
- Formal: "Sincerely," "Best regards,"
- Casual: "Thanks," "Best,"
- Personal: "Take care," "Cheers,"

### Professional Email

**Structure:**

```
Subject: {{PROF_SUBJECT}}

Dear {{TITLE}} {{LAST_NAME}},

I am writing regarding {{PURPOSE}}.

{{DETAILS}}

{{CALL_TO_ACTION}}

Thank you for your time.

{{CLOSING}},
{{YOUR_NAME}}
{{YOUR_TITLE}}
{{PHONE}}
{{EMAIL}}
```

**Example:**

```
Subject: Application for Sales Position

Dear Ms. Johnson,

I am writing to apply for the Sales Associate position 
advertised on your website.

I have 5 years of sales experience and excellent 
customer service skills. I have attached my resume 
for your review.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how I 
can contribute to your team.

Thank you for your consideration.

Best regards,
Maria Garcia
(555) 123-4567
maria.garcia@email.com
```

## Email Etiquette

### DO's

✅ Use clear subject lines
✅ Proofread before sending
✅ Reply within 24-48 hours
✅ Use "Reply All" sparingly
✅ Be polite
✅ Use professional tone for work
✅ Sign your name

### DON'Ts

❌ Use ALL CAPS (shouting)
❌ Send when angry
❌ Forward chain emails
❌ Share private information
❌ Use text abbreviations (LOL, OMG)
❌ Forget attachments
❌ Reply to spam

### Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Better Way |
|---------|-----------|
| Vague subject | Specific subject |
| Typos | Proofread first |
| No greeting | Include greeting |
| No signature | Add signature |
| Wrong recipient | Check carefully |

## Email Safety

### Spam

**Signs:**
- Unknown sender
- Too-good-to-be-true offers
- Urgent requests
- Spelling errors
- Suspicious links

**Protection:**
- Don't click unknown links
- Don't download suspicious attachments
- Use spam filter
- Mark spam when found
- {{SPAM_TIPS}}

### Phishing

**What:** Fake emails trying to get personal info

**Signs:**
- Asks for password
- Threatens account closure
- Links to fake websites
- Spelling errors
- Generic greeting

**Protection:**
- Never give password
- Check sender address
- Hover over links before clicking
- Contact company directly
- {{PHISHING_TIPS}}

### Privacy

**Keep Private:**
- Passwords
- Social Security Number
- Bank account numbers
- Credit card info
- {{PRIVATE_INFO}}

**Think Before Sending:**
- Can this be forwarded?
- Would I want this public?
- Is this appropriate?
