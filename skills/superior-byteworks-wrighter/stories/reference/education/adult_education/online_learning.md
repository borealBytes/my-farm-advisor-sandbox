## Asking for Help

### When to Ask

**Ask Immediately:**
- Technical problems
- Can't access materials
- Confused about instructions
- Falling behind

**Ask Early:**
- Don't understand concept
- Struggling with assignment
- Personal issues affecting studies
- Need accommodations

**How to Ask:**
1. Check syllabus first
2. Check discussion board
3. Email instructor
4. Attend office hours
5. Ask classmates

### Email Template

```
Subject: [Course] Question about [Topic]

Could you help me with [specific request]?

Thank you,
[Your Name]
[Student ID]
```
