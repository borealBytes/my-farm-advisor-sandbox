### Challenge 2: Procrastination

**Why Adults Procrastinate:**
1. Perfectionism ("I need 3 hours to do this right")
2. Overwhelm ("This is too much to start")
3. Fear of failure ("What if I can't do it?")
4. Competing priorities ("Work is more urgent")

**Anti-Procrastination Techniques:**

**The 5-Minute Rule:**
- Commit to just 5 minutes of studying
- Set a timer
- After 5 minutes, you can stop... or continue
- {{FIVE_MIN_WHEN}} will be your 5-minute time

**The Pomodoro Technique:**
1. Work for 25 minutes (one "pomodoro")
2. Take a 5-minute break
3. After 4 pomodoros, take a 15-30 minute break

## Quick Reference: 10 Time Management Rules for Adult Learners

1. ⏰ **Protect your study time** - Treat it like a job interview
2. 📅 **Plan weekly** - Sunday evening planning session
3. 🎯 **Set SMART goals** - Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
4. ⏱️ **Use timers** - 25-minute focused work blocks
5. 📵 **Remove distractions** - Phone away, notifications off
6. 🧠 **Match energy to task** - Hard work when you're alert
7. 👨‍👩‍👧 **Communicate with family** - They can't support what they don't know
8. 🔄 **Review and adjust** - What worked? What didn't?
9. 🏆 **Reward yourself** - Celebrate small wins
10. 💪 **Progress, not perfection** - 60% of plan beats 0% perfect
