## Purpose

This policy defines retention periods and disposal methods for company data.

---

## Retention Schedule

| Data Type | Retention Period | Disposition | Legal Basis |
|-----------|------------------|-------------|-------------|
| Financial records | 7 years | Archive then destroy | Tax regulations |
| Employee records | Term + 7 years | Archive then destroy | Labor laws |
| Customer contracts | Term + 7 years | Archive then destroy | Business needs |
| Email | 3 years | Delete | Policy |
| Security logs | 1 year | Delete | Compliance |
| Backup tapes | 30 days | Overwrite | Operational |
| Personnel files | Term + 7 years | Archive then destroy | Legal requirements |
| Payroll records | 7 years | Archive then destroy | Tax regulations |
| Legal documents | Per litigation hold | Varies | Legal hold |
| Marketing data | 3 years | Delete | Policy |

---

## Data Categories

### Financial Data
- General ledger
- Accounts payable/receivable
- Tax returns
- Payroll records

### Human Resources Data
- Employment applications
- Personnel files
- Performance reviews
- Benefits enrollment

### Customer Data
- Contracts
- Correspondence
- Transaction records
- Support tickets

### Operational Data
- System logs
- Backups
- Temporary files
- Working documents

---

## Disposal Methods

| Media | Method | Verification |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| Paper | Shredding | Certificate |
| Electronic | Secure deletion | Log entry |
| Hardware | Destruction | Certificate |
| Cloud | Data deletion | Vendor confirmation |

---

## Exceptions

Litigation hold: Suspend deletion when legal hold issued.

---

## Compliance

Failure to follow may result in disciplinary action.
