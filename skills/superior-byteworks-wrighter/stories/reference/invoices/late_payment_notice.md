## Notice Level — First Notice (1–30 Days Past Due)

This is a courtesy reminder that payment has not been received by the original due date. We understand that oversights occur, and we kindly request that you remit payment at your earliest convenience.

**Action Required:**

- Please remit payment of **$[X,XXX.XX]** by **[DD-MMM-YYYY]**
- If payment has already been sent, please disregard this notice and provide remittance confirmation to [ar@company.com]
- To arrange a payment plan, contact our AR department at [phone/email]

---

## Notice Level — Second Notice (31–60 Days Past Due)

Despite our previous correspondence dated [DD-MMM-YYYY], the above invoice(s) remain unpaid. Late fees of **[X]% per month** have begun accruing on the outstanding balance in accordance with the Payment Terms Policy (FIN-PT-[XXX]) and your executed service agreement.

**Action Required:**

- Remit full payment of **$[X,XXX.XX]** (including accrued late fees) by **[DD-MMM-YYYY]**
- Failure to remit payment by the above date will result in your account being placed on **credit hold**
- While on credit hold, no new orders will be processed until the balance is resolved
- Contact [AR Manager Name] at [phone/email] to discuss payment arrangements

---

## Notice Level — Final Notice (61–90 Days Past Due)

This constitutes our **final demand for payment** prior to referral for collections action. Your account balance of **$[X,XXX.XX]** is now **[X] days past due** and your account has been placed on credit hold.

**Action Required:**

- Remit full payment of **$[X,XXX.XX]** within **10 business days** of the date of this notice
- Failure to remit payment will result in one or more of the following actions:
  - Referral to a third-party collections agency
  - Reporting of delinquency to commercial credit bureaus
  - Initiation of legal proceedings to recover the outstanding balance, including reasonable attorney fees and court costs
- All collection costs incurred will be added to your outstanding balance

## Dispute Process

If you believe any portion of this balance is in error, you must submit a written dispute within **15 business days** of the date of this notice. Disputes should be directed to:

- **Email:** [disputes@company.com]
- **Mail:** [Company Name], Attn: Billing Disputes, [Address]
- **Reference:** Invoice Dispute Resolution Process (FIN-DR-[XXX])

Disputed amounts will not accrue additional late fees while under active, good-faith investigation. Undisputed amounts remain due per the original terms.

---

## Payment Arrangement Request

If you are experiencing temporary financial difficulty, we are willing to discuss a structured payment arrangement. Please contact our AR department within **5 business days** of this notice to request a Payment Plan Agreement. Payment plans are subject to approval by the [AR Manager / Director of Finance] and may include:

- Extended payment schedule (up to [X] months)
- Partial late fee waiver upon completion of the plan
- Continued service during the payment plan period (at management discretion)
