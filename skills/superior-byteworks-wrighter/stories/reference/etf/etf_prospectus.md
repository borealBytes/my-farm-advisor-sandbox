The Fund's NAV is calculated as of the close of regular trading on the {{EXCHANGE}} (normally {{NAV_TIME}} ET) on each day the Exchange is open for trading. The NAV is computed by dividing the total value of the Fund's assets, less liabilities, by the total number of Shares outstanding.

**Valuation of Portfolio Securities**: Portfolio securities are valued at the last sale price or official closing price. Securities for which no sales are reported are valued at the mean between the bid and ask price. Securities for which market quotations are not readily available are valued at fair value as determined in good faith by the Adviser under procedures approved by the Board, in accordance with Rule 2a-5 under the 1940 Act.

## Tax Considerations

### Federal Income Tax

The Fund intends to qualify each year for treatment as a "regulated investment company" under Subchapter M of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended (the "Code"). If so qualified, the Fund generally will not be subject to federal income tax on the portion of its investment company taxable income and net capital gain that it distributes to shareholders.

**Distributions**: Distributions of the Fund's investment company taxable income (other than qualified dividend income) will generally be taxable to shareholders as ordinary income. Distributions of net capital gain (the excess of net long-term capital gain over net short-term capital loss) will be taxable to shareholders as long-term capital gain, regardless of how long a shareholder has held Shares.

**Sale of Shares**: The sale of Shares is a taxable event. Shareholders will generally recognize gain or loss on the sale equal to the difference between the sale price and the shareholder's tax basis in the Shares sold.

**In-Kind Redemptions**: Because the Fund may satisfy redemption requests through in-kind transfers of securities, the Fund may realize fewer capital gains than a fund that meets redemptions in cash, potentially enhancing tax efficiency.

**Medicare Tax**: Certain shareholders may be subject to a 3.8% Medicare contribution tax on net investment income, including Fund distributions and gains on the sale of Shares.

**Tax Reporting**: The Fund will report to shareholders and the IRS the amount and character of distributions on Form 1099-DIV.
